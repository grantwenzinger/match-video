## [0.1.0] - 2021-11-24
### Added
- Methods for reading and writing anchors to video files.
- Methods for getting clips from a match video with set anchors.
- Command-line interface for reading and writing anchors.
- Unit tests.
- Examples showing how to get and use clips in scripts, Jupyter notebooks, and Streamlit apps.
